About Worm Ciliopathy Orthologs

Caenorhabditis elegans is a powerful model organism for studying ciliopathies due to its simple nervous system, transparent body, and well-characterized genetics.

Worm orthologs provide insights into fundamental ciliary mechanisms and serve as excellent models for high-throughput genetic screens.
